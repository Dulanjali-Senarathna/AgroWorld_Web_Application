Instructions For Farmers

  2. Instructions

View Temperature

  • You can view your cultivation's temperature value by just click on the Dashboard button.

Analise Temperature

  • Also you can view your previous temperature data for get an idea about average temperature around your cultivation by clicking Temperature button.

View Humidity

  • You can view your cultivation's humidity value by just click on the Dashboard button.

Analise Humidity

  • Also you can view your previous humidity data for get an idea about average humidity around your cultivation by clicking Humidity button.

View Soil Moisture

  • You can view your cultivation's soil moisture value by just click on the Dashboard button.

Analise Soil Moisture

  • Also you can view your previous humidity data for get an idea about average soil moisture of your cultivation by clicking Soil Moisture button.

Irrigation

  • This system is fully automated including irrigation. But if you want to handle your irrigation , you can do it by on or off water pump.
